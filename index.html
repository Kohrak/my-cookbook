Stuffed Arepa with Tuna Salad
01
713+ kcal Protein: 49g Carbs: 74g Fat: 24g

Ingredients

  • 80g Maize flour
  • 160g Tinned tuna in water (1½ tins)
  • 35g Greek yoghurt, 2% fat (~2.5 tbsp)
  • 20g Olive oil (~1 tbsp)
  • 35g Tinned sweetcorn
  • 35g Yellow onion (¼ pc)
  • 35g Tomatoes (½ pc)
  • 1 tsp Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the arepa dough with water and maize flour.
  2. Mix the tinned tuna with a little oil or mayonnaise.
  3. Prepare the filling with tuna, sweetcorn, onion, tomato, Greek yoghurt, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cook the arepa in a pan, flipping until cooked through, then stuff it.
Baked Hake with Potatoes
02
606+ kcal Protein: 45g Carbs: 61g Fat: 19g

Ingredients

  • 175g Hake, raw
  • 255g Medium potatoes (2 pcs)
  • 90g Yellow onion (1 pc)
  • 135g Broccoli (¼ head)
  • 135g Green pepper (1 pc)
  • 15g Olive oil (~1 tbsp)
  • ½ tbsp Lemon juice
  • ½–1 tsp Dill, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (gas) or 180°C (fan). Peel and wash the potatoes, cut into cubes, and spread on a lined baking tray.
  2. Add the vegetables and hake, then drizzle over the oil and lemon juice. Season with salt, pepper, and dill.
  3. Bake for 30–40 minutes until tender and golden.
Crispy Quesadilla (Air Fryer)
03
727+ kcal Protein: 56g Carbs: 71g Fat: 23g

Ingredients

  • 155g Minced beef, 3–7% fat
  • 95g Wholemeal tortilla wraps (2 pcs)
  • 30g Grated mozzarella, 24–25% fat
  • 40g Plain 0% fat yoghurt (~2.5 tbsp)
  • 45g Tomato sauce
  • 45g Tomatoes (½ pc)
  • 15g Iceberg lettuce (1 leaf)
  • 20g Salted corn tortilla chips
  • 1–2 tsp Taco seasoning
  • 1–2 seconds Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 180°C. Cut the lettuce into strips and the tomato into cubes.
  2. Fry the minced beef in a pan with cooking spray until browned. Season with taco seasoning.
  3. Assemble: cover half a tortilla with half the cheese. Spread yoghurt and top with lettuce, tomatoes, and tomato sauce. Add the beef and chips. Cover with the remaining cheese and fold the tortilla closed.
  4. Grease the air fryer basket and cook for 5–7 minutes until golden.
Chicken Pops
04
727+ kcal Protein: 50g Carbs: 74g Fat: 25g

Ingredients

  • 190g Chicken breast, raw
  • 65g Corn Flakes
  • 35g Whole egg (1 pc)
  • 15g Olive oil (~1 tbsp)
  • 30g Ketchup (~2 tbsp)
  • 60g White onion (¼ pc)
  • 65g Lettuce (6 leaves)
  • 125g Tomatoes (1½ pcs)
  • 1–2 pinches each Garlic powder, parsley, smoked paprika

Instructions

  1. Blitz the chicken in a blender and shape into small balls (pops).
  2. Set out two bowls: one with beaten egg and spices, one with crushed Corn Flakes.
  3. Dip each pop in the egg, shake off the excess, then coat in the Corn Flakes.
  4. Place in the air fryer and spray with half the oil. Cook at 180°C for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Use the remaining oil for the side salad. Serve with ketchup.

Mexican-Style Chicken Tostadas
06
594+ kcal Protein: 41g Carbs: 57g Fat: 22g

Ingredients

  • 130g Chicken breast, raw
  • 85g Gluten-free tortilla wraps (1½ pcs)
  • 30g High-protein grated cheese
  • 25g Fresh avocado (¼ pc)
  • 25g Greek yoghurt, 10% fat
  • 90g Tomatoes (1 pc)
  • 35g White onion (½ pc)
  • 20g Lettuce (2 leaves)
  • 5g Olive oil (~1 tsp)
  • 5ml Lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Chilli and garlic sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken in oil over a low heat, then shred it. In the same pan, cook the diced tomato and chopped onion with garlic. Mix in the shredded chicken.
  2. Avocado cream: mash together the yoghurt, fresh avocado, and lemon juice until smooth.
  3. Toast the tortillas in a dry pan until golden.
  4. Assemble: spread avocado cream on each tortilla, top with the chicken mix, lettuce, and grated cheese.
Prawn Rice Bowl
07
720+ kcal Protein: 51g Carbs: 72g Fat: 24g

Ingredients

  • 265g Raw prawns, frozen
  • 80g Basmati rice, raw
  • 65g Fresh avocado (¼ pc)
  • 65g White onion (¾ pc)
  • 65g Cherry tomatoes (4 pcs)
  • 15g Light mayonnaise, 26–28% fat (~2.5 tsp)
  • 5g Olive oil (~1.5 tsp)
  • 5ml Lemon juice
  • To taste Garlic, parsley, pepper, oregano, paprika

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Marinate the prawns in oil and spices. Cook in a pan over a medium heat for a couple of minutes each side. Set aside.
  3. Chop and mix the avocado, tomatoes, onion, and garlic. Add parsley, lemon juice, and salt.
  4. Assemble the bowl with rice, the vegetable mix, and prawns. Top with light mayonnaise.
Creamy Pasta Alfredo
08
720+ kcal Protein: 55g Carbs: 71g Fat: 23g

Ingredients

  • 90g Pasta, raw
  • 65g Chicken breast, raw
  • 65g Cottage cheese, 4% fat
  • 40g Parmesan, 28–30% fat
  • 70g Whole egg (1 pc)
  • 1 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta and reserve some of the cooking water. Cook the chicken in a separate pan.
  2. In the pasta pot with the reserved water, add the cottage cheese, Parmesan, garlic, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Stir in the cooked chicken. Remove from the heat.
  4. Stir in the egg yolk (or allow the egg white to cook through first before mixing in the yolk). Serve immediately.
Gnocchi with Chicken and Cream
09
720+ kcal Protein: 52g Carbs: 68g Fat: 26g

Ingredients

  • 200g Potato gnocchi
  • 170g Chicken breast, raw
  • 70g Single cream, 15% fat (~70ml)
  • 30g Bacon rashers (1 slice)
  • 15g Parmesan, 28–30% fat (~2 tbsp)
  • 1 pinch Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the gnocchi until they float.
  2. Cut the bacon into cubes and cook in a pan until golden. Remove. Cook the chicken breast in the same pan.
  3. Add the gnocchi and cream to the pan with the chicken. Simmer on a low heat for 3 minutes.
  4. Serve topped with bacon and Parmesan.
Chunky Potatoes with Bacon
10
600+ kcal Protein: 44g Carbs: 59g Fat: 20g

Ingredients

  • 320g Medium potatoes (2½ pcs)
  • 185g Turkey bacon (7 rashers)
  • 75g Skimmed whipped fresh cheese, 0% fat
  • 15g Spring garlic (1 pc)
  • 15g Olive oil (~2.5 tsp)
  • ½–1 handful Fresh thyme

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (gas) or 180°C (fan).
  2. Slice the potatoes into 5mm rounds. Toss with oil, salt, and pepper. Bake on a lined tray for 25 minutes.
  3. Cut the bacon into cubes and the spring garlic into rings.
  4. Remove the tray, top the potatoes with the whipped fresh cheese, bacon, and spring garlic. Season with thyme.
  5. Bake for a further 20 minutes.

Lamb's Lettuce and Gnocchi Salad
12
720+ kcal Protein: 53g Carbs: 69g Fat: 25g

Ingredients

  • 155g Cooked chicken breast pieces
  • 120g Potato gnocchi
  • 175g Portobello mushrooms (2 pcs)
  • 105g Cherry tomatoes (7 pcs)
  • 105g Carrots (2 pcs)
  • 85g Lamb's lettuce
  • 20g Parmesan, 28–30% fat (~2.5 tbsp)
  • 10g Olive oil (~2.5 tsp)
  • 1–2 tbsp Balsamic vinegar
  • ½–1 tbsp Mustard

Instructions

  1. Cook the gnocchi. Slice the vegetables.
  2. Add all the solid ingredients to a bowl.
  3. Mix together the oil, mustard, and balsamic vinegar to make a vinaigrette.
  4. Pour over the salad, garnish with Parmesan, and serve.

Avocado Toast with Egg Whites
14
600+ kcal Protein: 65g Carbs: 44g Fat: 17g

Ingredients

  • 230g Plain cottage cheese, 0% fat
  • 190g Skimmed whipped fresh cheese, 0% fat
  • 170g Pasteurised egg whites (6 pcs)
  • 85g Fresh avocado (½ pc)
  • 60g Wholemeal bread (1 slice)

Instructions

  1. Scramble the egg whites in a non-stick pan over a medium-high heat with salt and pepper until cooked through.
  2. Scoop out the avocado flesh and slice.
  3. Toast the bread. Spread the cottage cheese and whipped fresh cheese on the toast, then top with avocado slices.
  4. Serve the loaded toast alongside the scrambled egg whites.
Spinach-Stuffed Chicken
15
600+ kcal Protein: 46g Carbs: 59g Fat: 19g

Ingredients

  • 180g Chicken breast, raw
  • 90g Frozen chopped spinach
  • 65g Basmati rice, raw
  • 35g Cream cheese, 11% fat (~2 tbsp)
  • 10g Olive oil (~2 tsp)
  • 1 tbsp Garlic powder
  • 1–2 tsp Onion powder
  • 1 tsp Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the spinach for 2 minutes, then drain thoroughly. Mix with the cream cheese, salt, and pepper.
  2. Butterfly the chicken breast (open it out flat like a book). Spread the spinach mixture in the centre and roll it closed. Secure with cocktail sticks.
  3. Season the outside and brush with oil.
  4. Air fry at 180°C for 20 minutes. Cook the rice while the chicken cooks.
Pitta Bread Pizza
16
606+ kcal Protein: 46g Carbs: 58g Fat: 20g

Ingredients

  • 125g Wholemeal pitta bread (2 pcs)
  • 90g Tinned tuna in water (1 tin)
  • 60g Tomato sauce (~55ml)
  • 55g Provolone or Gouda cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (gas) or 180°C (fan).
  2. Spread the tomato sauce over the pitta bread.
  3. Grate the cheese and drain the tuna. Arrange on top of the pitta and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bake on a lined tray for 10 minutes until the cheese has melted and the edges are crisp.
Honey Chicken and Almond Rice
17
600+ kcal Protein: 34g Carbs: 70g Fat: 20g

Ingredients

  • 100g Chicken breast, raw
  • 65g White rice, raw
  • 65g Liquid strawberry yoghurt, 0% fat (½ pot)
  • 20g Whole almonds, skin on (~2 tbsp)
  • 15g Honey (~2 tsp)
  • 10g Olive oil (~1.5 tsp)

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice according to the packet instructions.
  2. Coat the raw chicken breast evenly with honey.
  3. Fry the chicken in oil over a medium-high heat for about 10 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Slice and serve with the rice, almonds, and yoghurt on the side.
