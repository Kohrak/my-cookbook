## **1\. Arepa Rellena de Ensalada de Atún**

**713+ cal | Protein: 49g | Carbs: 74g | Fat: 24g**  
**📋 Ingredientes:**

* **80g** Harina de maíz (Corn flour)  
* **160g** Atún, en agua, en lata (1 ½ can)  
* **35g** Yogur griego, 2% de grasa (\~2.5 tbsp)  
* **20g** Aceite de oliva (\~1 tbsp)  
* **35g** Maíz, en conserva  
* **35g** Cebolla amarilla (¼ pc)  
* **35g** Tomates (½ pc)  
* **1 cucharadita** Pimienta negra

**🔪 Instrucciones:**

1. Prepare the arepa with water and corn flour.  
2. Mix the canned tuna with some oil or mayonnaise.  
3. Prepare the filling mix with tuna, corn, onion, tomato, Greek yogurt, salt, and pepper.  
4. Flip and turn the arepa in the pan to cook, then stuff it.

## **2\. Merluza al Horno con Patatas**

**606+ cal | Protein: 45g | Carbs: 61g | Fat: 19g**  
**📋 Ingredientes:**

* **175g** Merluza, cruda  
* **255g** Patata, mediana (2 pcs)  
* **90g** Cebolla amarilla (1 pc)  
* **135g** Brócoli (¼ head)  
* **135g** Pimiento verde (1 pc)  
* **15g** Aceite de oliva (\~1 tbsp)  
* **½ cucharada** Zumo de limón  
* **½-1 cucharadita** Eneldo, picado

**🔪 Instrucciones:**

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (gas) or 180°C (fan). Peel/wash potatoes, cut into cubes, and spread on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.  
2. Add the vegetables and hake, then add the oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and dill to taste.  
3. Bake for about 30-40 minutes until tender and golden.

## **3\. Quesadilla Crujiente (Freidora de Aire)**

**727+ cal | Protein: 56g | Carbs: 71g | Fat: 23g**  
**📋 Ingredientes:**

* **155g** Carne picada de vacuno, 3-7% de grasa  
* **95g** Tortilla mexicana integral (2 pcs)  
* **30g** Mozzarella rallada, 24-25% de grasa  
* **40g** Yogur natural 0% grasa (\~2.5 tbsp)  
* **45g** Salsa de tomate  
* **45g** Tomates (½ pc)  
* **15g** Lechuga iceberg (1 leaf)  
* **20g** Totopos de maíz, salados  
* **1-2 cucharaditas** Condimento para tacos  
* **1-2 segundos** Spray para cocinar

**🔪 Instrucciones:**

1. Preheat air fryer to 180°C. Cut lettuce into strips and tomato into cubes.  
2. Fry ground beef in a pan with cooking spray until browned. Season with taco seasoning.  
3. Assemble: Cover half a tortilla with half the cheese. Spread yogurt and cover with lettuce, tomatoes, and sauce. Add beef and chips. Cover with remaining cheese and close the tortilla.  
4. Grease air fryer basket and cook for 5-7 minutes until golden.

## **4\. Pops de Pollo**

**727+ cal | Protein: 50g | Carbs: 74g | Fat: 25g**  
**📋 Ingredientes:**

* **190g** Pechuga de pollo, cruda  
* **65g** Cereales de maíz/Corn Flakes  
* **35g** Huevo, entero (1 pc)  
* **15g** Aceite de oliva (\~1 tbsp)  
* **30g** Ketchup (\~2 tbsp)  
* **60g** Cebolla blanca (¼ pc)  
* **65g** Lechuga (6 leaves)  
* **125g** Tomates (1 ½ pc)  
* **1-2 pizcas** Ajo en polvo, Perejil, y Pimentón de la Vera

**🔪 Instrucciones:**

1. Mince the chicken in a blender and shape into small balls (pops).  
2. Prepare two bowls: one for beaten egg with spices, one for crushed corn flakes.  
3. Dip chicken pops in egg, shake off excess, and coat in corn flakes.  
4. Place in air fryer, spray with half the oil. Cook at 180°C for 15-20 mins.  
5. Use remaining oil for the side salad. Serve chicken with ketchup.

## **15\. Pollo Relleno de Espinacas**

**600+ cal | Protein: 46g | Carbs: 59g | Fat: 19g**  
**📋 Ingredientes:**

* **180g** Pechuga de pollo, cruda  
* **90g** Espinacas, troceadas congeladas  
* **65g** Arroz basmati, crudo

* **1-2 cucharadita** Cebolla en polvo  
* **1 cucharadita** Pimienta negra

**🔪 Instrucciones:**

1. Boil spinach for 2 minutes, then drain well. Mix with cream cheese, salt, and pepper.

3. Season the outside and brush with oil.  
4. Air fry at 180°C for 20 minutes. Cook rice while chicken cooks.
